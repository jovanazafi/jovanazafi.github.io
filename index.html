About

I am a sociologist with a passion for labor markets and computational social science. Currently, I work as a Research Associate at the Institute of Social Sciences in Belgrade, where I also serve as the Field Director for Serbia for the European Social Survey (ESS).

I completed my PhD in Sociology from the University of Belgrade, with a thesis on factors of job insecurity in a comparative European perspective. Before joining the ISS, I was a Research Intern at Tilburg University under Prof. Dr. Ruud Muffels. I hold a Master's degree in Comparative Social Research from the Higher School of Economics in Moscow (2019).

My research spans the sociology of work, social stratification, and political sociology, explored through survey methodology, multilevel models, structural equation models, and latent growth models. I am co-leading Serbia Compass, a pioneering longitudinal panel study, alongside Dr. Bojan Todosijević.
